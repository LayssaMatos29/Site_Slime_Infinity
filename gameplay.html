Gameplay

After a nuclear war, humanity was extinguished from planet Earth and a thousand years later a new species appeared, the Slimes.

Now they must fight monsters created from the radiation of the old war. To survive, Slimes need players' skill to lead them to victory.

Fighting:

In order to fight and play Slime Infinity you need to have exactly 2 Slimes in your wallet. To battle it will be used only one Slime of your choice. Each Slime will have cards with attack or defense abilities. Battles will be played in turns where the battle initiative is decided from the difference in Slime Speed. HP potions are special potions that restore the Slime's HP.

Rank:

The combination will be based on an Elo system. Such a system would allow players to match other players who have similar overall abilities, this would promote in-game fun and players could earn $Slim in in-game currency much more easily.

Cloning:

In the future the clone mode will be released, where people can sacrifice 2 Slime to merge them into a single body, making it more powerful and consequently increasing the stats (HP, Speed, Attack, Defense) of your Slime.

Creation and $LME:

Slimes can be created to generate new offspring. To avoid Slime hyperinflation, there is a maximum number of times a Slime can be created.

Creating a Slime costs a coin (LME) and some Token Slime Infinity$ (SLIM) depending on how many times the Slimes have been created. The portion of LME and SLIM is variable and subject to adjustment

How long does it take for a Slime to become an adult ?

Slimes take 5 days to reach maturity.

After 5 days, you can turn your egg into an adult Slime and view your Slime Status.

The $Lime currency (LME) will be acquired only by playing the PVP battles, PVE and daily missions.

The $Slime Infinity (SLIM) Governance Token will be purchased by purchasing through sales platforms such as PancackeSwap and Exchanges.
