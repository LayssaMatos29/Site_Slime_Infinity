Sustainability:

Macro Economy

The Slime Infinity Game will be part of an NFT standard full-interaction BlockChain network that allows buyers to retain their ownership rights to their original assets and allows for the monetization of virtual assets in the real world. Consequently, all game items will be converted to tokens and buyers retain full ownership of the items and independent sales rights, which will not be monopolized by the game developer. Thus, the resources and work invested by players and artists will be materialized forever in the real market. With the definition of legitimate assets, funds and fees; a self-sufficient and sustainable ecosystem is created, while game assets will be valued with usable and collectable value.

Our team intends to create a strong and lasting economy for the Slime Infinity Universe. For this we will be launching two fungible tokens, one that is deflationary and has finite stock, the Slime Infinity ($SLIM) and another that is inflationary and has infinite stock, the ($LIME).

Slime Infinity is a game that bets on strategy and technique, with unlimited possibilities for expansion to bring players all over the world a fun and rich experience. In addition, exceptional players will receive either $ (LIME) or $ (SLIM) tokens, setting a standard for friendly competition between players.

Our first available token will be the Slime Infinity ($SLIM).

Our purpose for the $SLIM is to be a governance symbol that represents the financial participation and voting power of investors.

Our team wants to introduce many other ways to interact with our games and universe. We will be launching our game mode and we want our players to be able to customize their Slimes and all future features. In order to support this burgeoning economy, we will release our second token with an infinite supply of LIME ($ LIME).

To ensure game balance and longevity, Slime Infinity will continue to maximize asset circulation to allow the game's economy to sustain itself. We will also bring many ways of burning the currency $ (LIME), such as using the $ (LIME) to create new Slime, Weapons, Cloning and much more.
