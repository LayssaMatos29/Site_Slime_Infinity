Slime Infinity

Slime Infinity is a universe inspired by the old DDTANK game. It will be deployed on the Block Chain network where anyone can earn tokens through skillful gameplay and contributions to the ecosystem. Players can battle, collect and craft items using their collected rewards.

Why do we start SLIME INFINITY ?

Our initial plan is to create a P2E game that is cost-effective for everyone, as we see the potential for a game with a lower cost of entry than those already on the market.

Play and Win:

▪ Competing in PVP battles to earn coins and leaderboard prizes.

▪ Acquiring $LIME (LME) in PVE / PVP to create new Slime species. The $(LME) purchased can be sold on exchanges such as PancakeSwap and Exchanges.

▪ Creation of weapons and sale on the market.

Our mission:

Slime Infinity was built with the goal of being fun and profitable. We want the world to know the wonders of Play2Earn games and how they can make a difference in people's lives by uniting fun with financial profitability.
