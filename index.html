About

SLIME INFINITY is a play to earn game that combines a battle system in which players must use strength, distance and combat accessories to produce different effects and be rewarded. SLIME INFINITY is a game operated within the BLOACK CHAIN compatible with the BINANCE SMART CHAIN network.

Gameplay

Play&Earn: will be rewarded in three ways. In PvP Arena, Adventure, Daily Quest mode.

PVP BATTLE

Fight with your SLIME against other players, be the winner in the arena and earn SLIME TOKEN.

ADVENTURE

Help the SLIME nation defeat Kronos' army and get rewarded.

BREED

Combine two SLIMES to breed your SLIME baby. Expand your army or sell it on the market.

EVOLUTION

Choose two of your SLIMES to sacrifice them and make them a higher level. Evolution will increase your stats and your chances of winning the battle against enemies.
